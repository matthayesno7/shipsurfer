🏄ShipSurfer
Beta guide

Paddle out 🏄

ShipSurfer takes an app you built with Claude and gets it live — GitHub repo, database, hosting and a domain — on your own accounts, without touching a terminal. During the beta it's free.

✓ What you need

  • →Node.js 18+ — nodejs.org
  • →A Claude Code setup on your Mac
  • →Accounts you connect once: GitHub, Railway, Supabase (free tiers are fine — other services coming soon)

1 Install ShipSurfer — one time

Tell Claude Code to install this:

curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/matthayesno7/shipsurfer/main/install.sh | bash

2 Ship your app

  • 1.In Claude Code, open your project (the folder with your app).
  • 2.Type: "ship this with ShipSurfer." ShipSurfer starts up and opens in your browser automatically.
  • 3.First time only: get your license (use test card 4242 4242 4242 4242, any future date + any 3 digits — no real charge), then connect GitHub, Railway & Supabase by clicking through.
  • 4.Pick a domain option and hit Ship. Watch the surfer — you'll get a live URL in a few minutes.

Coming back later? Just say "ship this with ShipSurfer" again — your license and connections are remembered.

🌊 Domain options

  • →Railway URL — instant, free (your-app.up.railway.app)
  • →Free you.shipsurfer.app subdomain — clean, free, HTTPS
  • →Buy your own domain — search and register right in the app

Hit a snag? Whatever error the app shows is specific — send it to us verbatim and we'll sort it. Feedback of any kind is hugely welcome.
